Privacy Policy — FanTalk

Last updated: December 9, 2025

FanTalk (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates an integrated communication platform that connects businesses and users through messaging channels such as WhatsApp, email, and webchat.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how users can request the deletion of their data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

1.1 Information provided directly by users

  • Name, email, phone number, company information.
  • Message content exchanged via integrated channels (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • Preferences, settings, and account details.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device information (browser, operating system).
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps).
  • Usage statistics and analytics.

1.3 Information collected through Meta/WhatsApp APIs

For WhatsApp integrations, we may collect:

  • WhatsApp user ID
  • Message metadata
  • Message content
  • Delivery statuses

We only access data strictly required for communication features.

2. How We Use the Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and operate the FanTalk platform.
  • Deliver messaging services between businesses and users.
  • Improve support, analytics, and platform performance.
  • Comply with technical, legal, or security requirements.
  • Authenticate access to the platform and prevent fraud.

We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Sharing

We may share data only with:

  • Service providers essential to platform operation (cloud hosting, APIs).
  • Meta services (WhatsApp Business API) when required for message delivery.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection terms.

4. User Data Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

To request data deletion, email us at: contato@fantalk.com.br

We will respond within 30 days and delete the data unless retention is required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement organizational and technical security measures to protect user data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Access control and authentication
  • Secure infrastructure and backups

However, no system is 100% secure, and users share information at their own risk.

6. International Data Transfer

Some data may be processed in external servers depending on our hosting providers. All transfers comply with applicable regulations.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
